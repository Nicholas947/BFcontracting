ABOUT US

YOUR TRUSTED TURN-KEY UTILITIES CONTRACTOR FOR ANY PROJECT

B&F Contracting, Inc is a privately-owned corporation in Arizona since 1990.

B&F is the leader in the installation, construction, repair, and rehabilitation of underground utilities in the Southwest. The B&F team has successfully managed and completed thousands of utility projects of different scopes and delivery methods since our inception in 1990, earning us the reputation as one of the Valley’s most reliable and trusted contractors. We focus on delivering a safe, innovative, high-quality, and best value approach to any project, keeping the impact on nearby communities and businesses to a minimum.

400+ EMPLOYEES

B&F is proud to have a team comprised of highly trained professionals in the Contracting, Engineering & Construction fields. We have more than sufficient crews to complete multiple projects concurrently, of all delivery methods, and throughout all of Arizona.

33+ YEARS OF EXPERIENCE

B&F’s expertise allows for a smooth transition between project phases, from Preconstruction - Construction - Final Phase. Our experience has allowed us to refine our processes and approach using lessons learned. We always prioritize collaboration & teamwork by creating a transparent, trustful relationship with our clients. We are committed to continue to offer the best customer service and high quality projects.

1,000+ PROJECTS COMPLETED

For over 33 years, we have performed the highest quality work for customers while maintaining the most robust customer and supplier relationships in the industry. We are able to self-perform the vast majority of our work, controlling schedule & budget, and we are committed to continuous improvement on our services and quality of work.
