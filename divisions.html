WET UTILITIES

B&F’s dedicated team has effectively managed and successfully completed thousands of wet utility projects since our inception in 1990. The B&F team excels at the installation of waterlines, sewer lines, storm drains, water main rehabilitation, storm water retention structures, and more. The B&F team combines expertise, and innovation to bring the most value and quality to each of our projects. Our extensive knowledge and industry experience in providing utility construction services for various agencies and municipalities throughout the Valley distinguishes us in the Arizona construction industry.

UTILITY REHABILITATION

B&F’s Utility Rehabilitation Division is recognized throughout the Southwest as a market leader in full service rehabilitation. Our expertise includes sewer pipeline rehabilitation projects of all sizes delivered through different procurement methods (CMAR, JOC, DB, etc.), emergency response work, and multiple large complex bypass projects. We can provide lining (CIPP, pressure lining, HDPE), pumping, flow monitoring, odor control and fusion capabilities. B&F has always been a leading adopter of innovative methods including one of the first to install Armorock, Predl, FRP Inserts, and complete in pipe and manhole Carbon fiber repairs to critical infrastructure.

DRY UTILITIES

B&F has the resources to complete work related to the installation and infrastructure of communication services and networks, fiber optic installation, and electrical for industrial, commercial, and residential projects. We are able to self-perform the majority of the scope on dry utility projects due to our large inventory of resources and equipment, which gives us the ability to control and accelerate project schedule while maintaining higher quality levels. Additionally, our experience allows us to properly assess conflicting conditions with utilities and develop innovative solutions and approaches that benefit our clients.

TRAFFIC SIGNALS & STREET LIGHTS

B&F’s Traffic Signals & Street Lights Division has varied experience in all types of streetlights, traffic signals, and traffic intersection modifications. This experience varies from full new street signalization and installation to modifications and updates to existing signalization in smaller roads and major arterial intersections. Our good standing with local power companies and continuous education regarding technical installation requirements and traffic control procedures allow us to provide high-quality products and services to our clients.

PUMP SOLUTIONS

As the leader in the installation, construction, repair, and rehabilitation of underground utilities in the Southwest, we deeply understand contractors and their needs for specialized equipment and materials. B&F’s Pump Solutions & Rentals Division provides the specialty equipment and materials you need to complete your project 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
We are proud owners of a diverse fleet of equipment, materials, and tools for different scopes of work, including pumping equipment, HDPE fusion equipment, inspection equipment, slipping winches, trenchless equipment, concrete trucks, CCTV inspection for water and sewer, and more.
