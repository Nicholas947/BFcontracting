HARD BIDS

This is the ultimate procurement method for cost-conscious owners - hard bid contracts! With this approach, you can be assured that your construction project will be completed at the most cost-effective price. The competitive bidding process ensures that contractors are submitting their best prices, and you can choose the lowest qualified bidder for maximum savings. While hard bid contracts prioritize cost savings over collaboration, they remain a useful procurement method for many projects. However, it's important to be aware of the potential drawbacks, such as a lack of collaboration between the owner, design team, and contractor. Consider whether hard bid contracts are the best fit for your project's needs and budget. Choose the ultimate cost-effective option for your construction project - hard bid contracts are the best in the business!
